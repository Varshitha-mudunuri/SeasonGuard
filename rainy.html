Rainy Season Diseases

Information on common rainy season diseases, their symptoms, cures, medications, and preventive measures.

Common Rainy Season Diseases

Malaria

Symptoms

High fever, chills, sweating, headache, nausea, and fatigue.

Medication & Cure

Antimalarial drugs prescribed by a doctor.

Prevention

Use mosquito nets, insect repellents, and remove standing water.

Dengue Fever

Symptoms

High fever, severe headache, joint and muscle pain, rash.

Medication & Cure

Supportive care, hydration, and pain relievers as advised by a doctor.

Prevention

Avoid mosquito bites, remove water storage areas, and use repellents.

Leptospirosis

Symptoms

Fever, muscle pain, headache, red eyes, abdominal pain, vomiting.

Medication & Cure

Antibiotics and supportive treatment as prescribed.

Prevention

Avoid walking through floodwaters, wear protective footwear.

Cholera

Symptoms

Severe watery diarrhea, vomiting, dehydration, leg cramps.

Medication & Cure

Oral rehydration solution (ORS) and antibiotics in severe cases.

Prevention

Drink safe water, maintain food hygiene, and wash hands regularly.

Jaundice

Symptoms

Yellowing of skin and eyes, fatigue, dark urine, nausea.

Medication & Cure

Rest, hydration, and medications as recommended by a healthcare provider.

Prevention

Avoid contaminated water and maintain hygiene.

Viral Fever

Symptoms

Fever, body aches, fatigue, chills, runny nose, cough.

Medication & Cure

Rest, hydration, and over-the-counter medications for fever and pain.

Prevention

Avoid crowded places, practice good hygiene, and stay hydrated.

Cold and Flu

Symptoms

Runny nose, sore throat, fever, body aches, and cough.

Medication & Cure

Rest, fluids, over-the-counter cold remedies.

Prevention

Maintain hygiene, avoid close contact with infected individuals.

Typhoid

Symptoms

High fever, weakness, stomach pain, headache, diarrhea or constipation.

Medication & Cure

Antibiotics as prescribed by a doctor.

Prevention

Ensure clean drinking water and food hygiene.

Fungal Infections

Symptoms

Itchy red patches, peeling skin, rashes, and sometimes blisters.

Medication & Cure

Antifungal creams or medications as advised by a doctor.

Prevention

Keep skin dry, wear breathable clothing, avoid sharing personal items.

Stomach Infections

Symptoms

Abdominal pain, diarrhea, vomiting, fever, dehydration.

Medication & Cure

Hydration, rest, and prescribed antibiotics if necessary.

Prevention

Drink clean water, eat well-cooked food, and maintain hand hygiene.
